Given a sequence of integers. Find any of its longest increasing subsequences.

The sequence is given in the form of numbers separated by spaces on a single line.

Output the subsequence in a single line, space separated.


Sample Input:
1 2 3 4 5
Sample Output:
1 2 3 4 5

Sample Input:
1 4 4 3 5 2 8
Sample Output:
1 4 5 8

Sample Input:
1 3 5 2 4
Sample Output:
1 3 5


Memory limit: 256 Mb
Time limit: 5s
