There are two horses on a chess board and four coordintes \( x_1 \), \( y_1 \), \( x_2 \), \( y_2 \) are typed in. Determine, whether they can hit each other or not. 

INPUT

Four integer coordinates in the following sequence: \( x_1, y_1, x_2, y_2 \). The coordinates are not the same.

OUTPUT

"YES" (uppercase), if they hit each other and "NO" if they don't. 


Sample Input:
4 4 2 5
Sample Output:
YES

Sample Input:
4 4 6 6
Sample Output:
NO

Sample Input:
4 4 6 5
Sample Output:
YES


Memory limit: 256 Mb
Time limit: 5s
