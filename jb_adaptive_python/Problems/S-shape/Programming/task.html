Given two integers n and m, each of then does not exceed 30.. Fill in the two-dimensional array (matrix) of size n×m with the numbers from 1 to n×m in “S-shape”, as shown in the example.

Output data format

Output the resulting matrix, each element should take exactly 4 characters (including spaces).


Sample Input:
3 5
Sample Output:
1 2 3 4 5
10 9 8 7 6
11 12 13 14 15


Memory limit: 256 Mb
Time limit: 1s
