How to open this course in PyCharm Edu

1. Download and install PyCharm Edu.

2. Open PyCharm Edu, go to File > New Project... > Educational.

3. On the line "Courses:" click on the cogwheel icon, then choose "Login to Stepik". See screenshot below:

4. You will be asked for your Stepik email and password. If you don't know your password (e.g. you've used social network login to Stepik), you can reset the password on https://stepik.org/accounts/password/reset/ or set it on https://stepik.org/edit-profile/pass while logged in.

5. After entering your email and password, you may optionally setup master password to store your Stepik credentials locally in the IDE.

6. After some loading time, you'll be able to choose the Adaptive Python course from "Courses" dropdown menu. Then click "Create" button and you're ready to go!

Let's start! Solve this problem in PyCharm Edu.

Given the series of numbers: 1, -0.5, 0.25, -0.125, ... . Try to understand how it works.
On the input, the program has integer \( n \), and the output should be a real number corresponding to the sum of first \( n \) elements of the series.

Note:  if you have any problems with installing or logging in PyCharm Edu, please tell us about it in comments.


Sample Input:
5
Sample Output:
0.6875
